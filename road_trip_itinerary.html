DAY BY DAY ITINERARY

DAY 1 — Jun 18 Cleveland ✈ Denver ✈️ ~3.5h flight ▼

Activities

  • Morning flight CLE → DEN (United direct, ~3.5 hrs)
  • Check into hotel, drop bags, freshen up
  • Meet up with friends!
  • Explore the 16th Street Mall — free shuttle, shops, restaurants
  • Dinner with friends at a local favorite
  • Take it easy — you're at 5,280 ft, start hydrating!

Details

Flight: CLE → DEN, depart ~8am, arrive ~10:30am MT

Altitude: Denver is the Mile High City (5,280 ft)

Where we stay:

🏨 Embassy Suites by Hilton Denver Downtown

1420 Stout St — 2-room suites, complimentary breakfast, evening reception, indoor pool. Hilton Honors eligible.

🏙️16th Street Mall 🏨Embassy Suites 🌄Denver Skyline
DAY 2 — Jun 19 Denver — Visiting Friends 🏙️ City day ▼
🗺️ Route: Denver Area

Activities

  • Free hot breakfast at the Embassy Suites
  • Hang with friends — their pick for the day!
  • Denver Museum of Nature & Science (incredible dinosaur hall)
  • Red Rocks Park & Amphitheatre — free to walk around, stunning geology
  • Union Station for lunch — great food hall
  • Hotel pool time for the kids
  • Farewell dinner with friends

Denver Highlights

🎸 Red Rocks: Even without a concert, the amphitheatre and Trading Post Trail are spectacular

🏊 Hotel Pool: Embassy Suites has an indoor pool

💰 Budget Tip: Free breakfast + evening reception = save on two meals

Where we stay:

🏨 Embassy Suites Denver (Night 2)
🎸Red Rocks Amphitheatre 🦖Denver Museum 🚂Union Station
DAY 3 — Jun 20 Pick Up RV → Rocky Mountain NP 🚐 ~75 mi / 3 hrs ▼
🗺️ Route: Denver → RMNP → Grand Lake

Activities

  • Pick up RV at Cruise America Denver depot (9am)
  • RV orientation & walkthrough — learn the systems
  • Grocery stop at King Soopers — stock up!
  • Drive to Estes Park (gateway to RMNP, ~1.5 hrs)
  • Enter Rocky Mountain NP and drive Trail Ridge Road
  • Stop at Alpine Visitor Center (11,796 ft!)
  • Elk everywhere in the meadows — especially at dusk
  • Exit the west side and camp near Grand Lake

Rocky Mountain NP

⛰️ Trail Ridge Road: Highest continuous paved road in North America — peaks at 12,183 ft!

🦌 Wildlife: Elk, marmots, bighorn sheep, pikas

🎫 Timed Entry: Reserve in advance at recreation.gov

Where we stay:

🏕️ Elk Creek Campground, Grand Lake
🏔️Trail Ridge Road 🦌Elk Meadows ⛰️Alpine Visitor Center
DAY 4 — Jun 21 Grand Lake → Grand Teton (Big Drive) 🚐 ~480 mi / 7 hrs ▼
🗺️ Route: Grand Lake → Grand Teton NP

Activities

  • Early departure — big drive day through Wyoming!
  • Head north on US-40 → I-80 W through Rawlins, WY
  • Lunch stop in Rock Springs or Pinedale, WY
  • Scenery shifts from plains to mountain valleys
  • Arrive at Grand Teton NP by late afternoon
  • First views of the Teton Range — jaw-dropping
  • Set up camp, campfire dinner, early night

Road Trip Tips

🎵 Entertainment: Download playlists/audiobooks before leaving

🎮 Kids: License plate game, travel bingo

⛽ Fuel: Fill up in Rawlins and Rock Springs

Where we stay:

🏕️ Colter Bay RV Park, Grand Teton NP — full hookups
🏔️Teton Range First View 🌅Wyoming Open Road
DAY 5 — Jun 22 Grand Teton National Park 🏞️ Park day ▼
🗺️ Route: Grand Teton NP

Activities

  • Sunrise at Schwabacher Landing (iconic reflection shot!)
  • Boat shuttle across Jenny Lake → hike to Hidden Falls & Inspiration Point (2.4 mi)
  • Lunch on the Jenny Lake shore or at the Lodge
  • Drive the 42-mile Scenic Loop Road
  • Mormon Row historic barns — classic photo op
  • Evening: Oxbow Bend at sunset to spot moose, eagles, and beavers

Must-See Highlights

🦌 Wildlife: Moose, elk, bald eagles, bears!

📸 Photo Ops: Schwabacher, Mormon Row, Snake River Overlook

🥾 Kid Rating: Jenny Lake hike is perfect for ages 9+

Where we stay:

🏕️ Colter Bay RV Park (Night 2)
📸Schwabacher Landing 🌊Jenny Lake 🏚️Mormon Row Barns 🦌Oxbow Bend Moose
DAY 6 — Jun 23 Grand Teton → Yellowstone (South) 🚐 ~60 mi / 1.5 hrs ▼
🗺️ Route: Grand Teton → Old Faithful

Activities

  • Drive north into Yellowstone via the South Entrance
  • Stop at Lewis Falls
  • West Thumb Geyser Basin — geothermal pools on the lake
  • Watch Old Faithful erupt (every ~90 min)
  • Walk the Upper Geyser Basin boardwalk (Morning Glory Pool, Castle Geyser)
  • Old Faithful Inn — walk through the incredible log lobby

Yellowstone Fun Facts

🌋 Yellowstone sits on a supervolcano — 34×45 miles!

💨 Old Faithful erupts ~17 times per day

🐃 Home to ~5,000 free-roaming bison

Where we stay:

🏕️ Fishing Bridge RV Park, Yellowstone NP — full hookups
💨Old Faithful 🌈Grand Prismatic Spring ♨️Morning Glory Pool 🏨Old Faithful Inn
DAY 7 — Jun 24 Yellowstone — Grand Canyon & Lamar Valley 🏞️ Park drives ~60 mi ▼
🗺️ Route: Yellowstone Loop

Activities

  • Early morning: Hayden Valley for bison herds & possibly wolves
  • Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone — Artist Point waterfall view
  • Uncle Tom’s Trail (328 steps down to the falls!)
  • Norris Geyser Basin — hottest and most dynamic area
  • Grand Prismatic Spring overlook
  • Lamar Valley at dusk — “America’s Serengeti”

Details

📏 Lower Falls: 308 feet — twice Niagara!

🐺 Wildlife: Lamar Valley at dawn/dusk for wolves

⚠️ Stay 100 yards from bears/wolves, 25 yards from bison

Where we stay:

🏕️ Fishing Bridge RV Park (Night 2)
💧Lower Falls 🐺Lamar Valley Wildlife 🌋Norris Geyser Basin 🐃Bison Herds
DAY 8 — Jun 25 Yellowstone → Moab (Big Drive Day) 🚐 ~370 mi / 6.5 hrs ▼
🗺️ Route: Yellowstone → Moab

Activities

  • Depart early — exit Yellowstone via South Entrance
  • One last drive through Grand Teton (goodbye mountains!)
  • Lunch stop in Rock Springs, WY
  • Cross into Utah — landscapes shift to red rock desert
  • Scenic stretch on US-191 south into Moab
  • Arrive by late afternoon — swim at campground pool
  • Sunset at the Colorado River overlook

Road Trip Tips

🎵 Download playlists before leaving Yellowstone

⛽ Fill up in Rock Springs — long stretch to Moab

Where we stay:

🏕️ Sun Outdoors Arches Gateway (Moab)
DAY 9 — Jun 26 Arches National Park 🏞️ Park day ▼
🗺️ Route: Arches National Park

Activities

  • Enter Arches early (timed entry — book at recreation.gov!)
  • Balanced Rock — short walk, incredible photo op
  • Windows Section — North Window, South Window, Turret Arch (1 mi)
  • Delicate Arch hike — THE iconic Utah hike (3 mi, moderate)
  • Landscape Arch — longest arch in North America (1.6 mi, easy)
  • Evening: jet boat on the Colorado River or mini golf in Moab

Arches Fun Facts

🪨 2,000+ natural stone arches

📏 Landscape Arch spans 306 feet — longer than a football field!

🌡️ Hot! 2+ liters of water per person for Delicate Arch

Where we stay:

🏕️ Sun Outdoors Arches Gateway (Night 2)
🪨Delicate Arch 🌉Landscape Arch 🪨Balanced Rock 🖼️The Windows
DAY 10 — Jun 27 Canyonlands & Dead Horse Point 🏞️ ~30 mi from Moab ▼
🗺️ Route: Moab → Dead Horse Point → Canyonlands

Activities

  • Dead Horse Point State Park — one of the most photographed overlooks in the West
  • Drive into Canyonlands Island in the Sky district
  • Grand View Point — 100 miles of canyons
  • Mesa Arch hike (0.5 mi loop)
  • Upheaval Dome — meteorite or salt dome?
  • Back to Moab for pizza

Cool Facts

🎬 Dead Horse Point was in Thelma & Louise and Westworld

🔬 Scientists still debate Upheaval Dome’s origin!

Where we stay:

🏕️ Sun Outdoors Arches Gateway (Night 3)
🏜️Dead Horse Point 🌅Mesa Arch Sunrise 👁️Grand View Point
DAY 11 — Jun 28 Moab → Bryce Canyon (via Goblin Valley) 🚐 ~270 mi / 4 hrs ▼
🗺️ Route: Moab → Goblin Valley → Bryce Canyon

Activities

  • Pack up Moab — say goodbye to red rock country
  • Detour: Goblin Valley State Park — kids go wild climbing the “goblins”
  • Basically a natural playground — budget 1-2 hours here
  • Continue south on scenic UT-12 — “one of America’s most beautiful roads”
  • Lunch in the tiny town of Torrey or Boulder
  • Arrive Bryce Canyon by mid-afternoon
  • Walk the Rim Trail at sunset — hoodoos turn golden-red
  • Bryce Point and Inspiration Point

What’s a Hoodoo?

🗼 Tall rock spires formed by frost wedging and erosion

🏔️ Bryce is at 8,000-9,000 ft — cooler than Moab!

👾 Goblin Valley: Featured in Galaxy Quest!

Where we stay:

🏕️ Ruby's Inn RV Park, Bryce Canyon — full hookups
👾Goblin Valley 🗼Bryce Hoodoos 🌅Bryce Point Sunset 🛣️Scenic UT-12
DAY 12 — Jun 29 Bryce Canyon — Full Day! 🏞️ Park day ▼
🗺️ Route: Bryce Canyon

Activities

  • Sunrise at Sunrise Point
  • Navajo Loop + Queen’s Garden Trail combo (2.9 mi) — walk AMONG the hoodoos
  • #1 hike in the park — do it in the morning for best light
  • Ride the free park shuttle along the 18-mile scenic drive
  • Rainbow Point (highest point, 9,115 ft)
  • Afternoon: horseback ride along the canyon rim
  • Night sky ranger program — 7,500+ stars visible!
  • S’mores at camp 🔥

Bryce by the Numbers

🗼 Largest concentration of hoodoos on Earth

🎨 Colors come from iron and manganese in the rock

🐴 Horseback rides: ~$80/person for 1.5 hrs

⭐ One of the darkest skies in North America

Where we stay:

🏕️ Ruby's Inn RV Park (Night 2)
🗼Navajo Loop Trail 🌅Sunrise Point ⭐Night Sky Program 🐴Horseback Riding
DAY 13 — Jun 30 Bryce → Zion National Park 🚐 ~75 mi / 1.5 hrs ▼
🗺️ Route: Bryce Canyon → Zion

Activities

  • Mossy Cave Trail (0.8 mi) — waterfall + small cave
  • Drive to Zion via UT-9 through the Zion-Mt. Carmel Tunnel
  • Arrive by late morning — set up camp
  • Riverside Walk (2.2 mi, easy) → wade into The Narrows
  • Bring water shoes — ankle to knee deep in the Virgin River!
  • Pa’rus Trail (3.5 mi, flat, paved) — riverside stroll
  • Watch sunset light up the 2,000-ft canyon walls

Zion Details

🚌 Free shuttle system (no private vehicles in canyon)

👟 Bring water shoes! Essential for The Narrows

🌡️ Canyon floor can hit 100°F+

💡 No rush — all afternoon to explore + tomorrow morning too

Where we stay:

🏕️ Watchman Campground, Zion NP
🌊The Narrows 🛣️Zion Tunnel 🌅Canyon Walls Sunset
DAY 14 — Jul 1 Zion Morning → Las Vegas! 🚐 ~160 mi / 2.5 hrs ▼
🗺️ Route: Zion → Las Vegas

Activities

  • Early morning: Emerald Pools Trail (Lower 1.2 mi, Upper add 1 mi)
  • Canyon Overlook Trail (1 mi) — panoramic views
  • Late morning: pack up RV → Las Vegas!
  • Drop off RV at Cruise America Las Vegas (~3pm)
  • Uber to Mandalay Bay Resort
  • Afternoon: Mandalay Bay Beach pool complex (wave pool + lazy river!)
  • Evening walk on The Strip — Bellagio Fountains at night

Welcome to Vegas!

🏊 Mandalay Bay Beach: 11-acre pool with wave pool, lazy river, sandy beach

🍣 Try the Burger Bar or House of Blues at Mandalay Bay

Where we stay:

🏨 Mandalay Bay Resort & Casino
💧Emerald Pools 🏊Mandalay Bay Beach ⛲Bellagio Fountains
DAY 15 — Jul 2 Las Vegas — Full Day of Fun! 🎰 Vegas day! ▼
🗺️ Route: The Las Vegas Strip

Activities

  • Sleep in — you’ve earned it!
  • Shark Reef Aquarium at Mandalay Bay — sharks, rays, sea turtles
  • Pool time at Mandalay Bay Beach (wave pool + lazy river)
  • Walk The Strip — M&M’s World, Venetian canals
  • 🎩 Mac King Comedy Magic Show (Excalibur, ~4pm — all ages!)
  • Dinner: Secret Pizza at The Cosmopolitan (hidden speakeasy-style!)
  • High Roller Observation Wheel at night (550 ft!)
  • Bellagio Fountains for a grand finale

Vegas with Kids!

🎩 Mac King: Comedy magic, all ages, ~$35-50/ticket

🎡 High Roller: 30-min ride, ~$25/adult, ~$15/child

🦈 Shark Reef: Walk under a shark tank! ~$30/adult, ~$25/child

🍕 Secret Pizza: 3rd floor of Cosmopolitan, follow the vinyl records — no sign!

Where we stay:

🏨 Mandalay Bay Resort (Night 2)
🦈Shark Reef Aquarium 🎡High Roller Wheel 🎰The Strip at Night 🍕Secret Pizza
