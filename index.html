DAY 12 — Tue Jun 22 Cody → Thermopolis → Lander 🚐 ~200 mi / 4 hrs ▼
🗺️ Route: Cody → Thermopolis → LanderOpen in Google Maps ↗

Activities

  • Morning: one more stop at Buffalo Bill Center if kids want
  • Drive south to Hot Springs State Park in Thermopolis, WY (~85 mi, 1.5 hrs)
  • Swim at the State Bath House — free! (yes, really free) Home to the world's largest mineral hot springs
  • Kids will love the hot soak + the (small) water slides at nearby commercial pools

Thermopolis Magic

🕍 The State Bath House maintains a 104°F pool open to everyone for free — it’s written into the 1896 treaty with the Shoshone and Arapaho that bought the springs

🦣 The state park even has a small wild bison herd to spot
